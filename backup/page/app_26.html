Description

BDO's digital payment app that makes everyday payments as easy as 1-2 tap!


Scan to Pay in Stores, Send and Request Money, Pay Bills and more, all conveniently through your mobile phone.



SEND MONEY TO OTHER BANKS AND MOBILE WALLETS

Send Money to other banks and mobile wallets in just a few taps via InstaPay! Just enter your receiver’s details, and Send Money

straight from your linked BDO accounts.



SEND MONEY FOR FREE TO BDO ACCOUNTS

If they're on BDO Pay, just use their number or look for their name in your phone's contacts. Or copy-paste an account number and

tap Send!



PAY BILLS WITHOUT ENROLLING BILLERS

Pay your bills safely from the comfort of your home. No need to enroll billers - just enter your bill details and hit Pay! Enjoy new

billers added every week.



GO CASHLESS ONLINE OR IN-STORE

Whether you’re paying online or in-store, make it safe and convenient with BDO Pay’s cashless QR payments. Enjoy BDO Pay’s

growing network of in-store QR merchants.



SPLIT BILLS AND REQUEST YOUR FRIENDS’ SHARE IN ONE TAP

Make it easy to split group payments with BDO Pay! Divide the bill evenly with up to five other people or request different

amounts per person, and get paid straight to your account. No need to count cash or find change!



REQUEST MONEY LIKE A PRO

Asking someone to pay you back is now digital with the BDO Pay app. When someone receives your request, all they have to do is

tap Send, and the amount goes straight into your deposit account.

Refreshing but

Honestly I think this is a great app, but I can’t understand why not just focus your effort and resources on your existing BDO personal app? This whole app can just be integrated inside BDO Personal or better yet, make this app have all the features from BDO Personal (Specifically transfer to other banks function). In that way, users won’t have to switch apps just to pay or transfer funds.


Online banking is supposed to be easy and seamless not the other way around. I hope you read this and consider and I’ve read same sentiments in the reviews as well.


Focus on one app and don’t make separate apps that can be just a function inside one. People are getting confused.

JeffJQ , 22/04/2021

Not working for me.

I think the reason why BDO made this app is that they want to compete with the mobile wallet apps like GCASH and PAYMAYA. While it is high time that they have their own Mobile Wallet, I think they should also focus their efforts in improving their online banking app. The interface is ugly to be honest. Additionally, imagine enrolling your own BDO Credit Card under the third party Biller/Company even if the card is already enrolled in your mobile banking. Why can’t they just simply add a pay button under the credit card details page? With other banks, it don’t take several days before the payment coming from your own account is reflected. Here they’re treating it as if the payment was made using a third party channel.


Going back to BDO PAY, it is still not working for me after a couple of days. Even tried re-installing the app.


Being the number 1 bank in the country, I think BDO should put more efforts in improving its online services.
