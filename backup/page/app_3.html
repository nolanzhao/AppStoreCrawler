Description

Bank wherever you are with the BPI app. Get the same digital banking experience of BPI Online straight from your pocket.


Download the app today and power up your digital lifestyle. With the app, you can:


• Secure your transactions using biometrics with the Mobile Key, your new alternative to the One-Time PIN via SMS.

• Get an instant and detailed view of your accounts and one-tap shortcuts to access your bank statements.

• Send money in many ways - to other BPI accounts and other banks via InstaPay. Scan and pay is an even quicker method, when you create your personalized QR code for error-free transfers.

• Pay bills to more than 650 billers.

• Access our loading hub and load any of our partners for prepaid phones, utilities and prepaid card. You can also top-up your e-wallet for any of our growing list partners to complement your cashless travel and digital lifestyle needs.

• Set your debit card’s limits for purchase and withdrawal with ease with card control.


Download the app. Go digital today.

Lock/Unlock Option in CREDIT CARDS

The current BPI app has an existing option to temporarily block debit cards or “Lock and Unlock” it. Other banks like BDO and Citibank customers can Lock and Unlock their respective CREDIT cards VIA THEIR APP. I do not see the point of not having this feature (to LOCK temporarily credit cards) on BPI app. Please include this on your next app update. We do not want the hassle of calling your customer support in case the need arises. And we don’t want the constant anxiety of having unexpected charges on our credit cards, knowing that there are different ways to hack finance-related info. We WANT a easy way to DISABLE the purchasing ability of BPI credit cards if they are not in use. It will be more convenient for both of us to have this very essential feature I’m talking about. Anyway, it is not our money that is at stake in credit cards. IT’S YOURS. Then do things to protect “your” money from thieves. Let’s help each other please. Include Lock/Unlock CREDIT CARDS option in BPI app ASAP, hopefully before Christmas 2020.
