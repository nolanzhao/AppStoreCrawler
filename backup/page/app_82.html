How are your privacy and permissions policies?


Pedicash values your privacy. The submitted photo of your government-issued ID and other personal information will be used to verify your credit profile on our system. We will NEVER share any of your information without your direct consent or explicit permission.


On the app, we will require the following permissions to verify your identity:

Location Access - To auto-verify your address.

Contacts Access - To easily nominate your contact references.

Camera Access - To scan your ID to complete ID verification


How safe are my personal details?

All personal details are fully secure and protected

Data privacy agreements are insisted upon registering

Routine assessments are done to ensure quality data protection


How and how fast can I receive the cash?

Approval of loan application can take upto 5 minutes to 24 hours

Upon approval, expect same-day disbursement through Skypay

Cash-out centers: RD Pawnshop and M Lhuillier

Other withdrawal channels: Bank Transfer, GCash or Coins.ph


How do I repay my loan?

Payment can be partial or in full, as long as it is within the loan period

You may check your loan term duration on the app

Pay through our select payment channels ONLY: RD Pawnshop, M Lhuillier, 7-Eleven, True Money, EC Pay and GCash
