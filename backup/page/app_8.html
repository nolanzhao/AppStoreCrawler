Description

Easy and convenient banking anywhere, all the time with the BDO Digital Banking App!

• Manage and keep tabs on all your accounts, including transactions from the past three months.

• Send money to any BDO account using account numbers or their linked mobile number or email address for free!

• Send money to other banks with InstaPay.

• Generate a QR code and use it to withdraw from QR-enabled ATMs! No card needed - just scan the code with your phone.

• Pay bills from your phone to over 600 merchants - no need to register billers in ATMs or branches.

• Reload your prepaid mobile SIM whether you’re a Globe, TM, SMART, or Talk and Text.

• Protect your accounts through security features like setting transaction limits or locking cards.

Download the BDO Digital Banking App and sign up today!
