Description

Your partner for cash loans and installment shopping


Cashalo is your financial partner, whether for urgent needs or to shop for the Iatest gadgets. It is designed for Filipinos, by Filipinos. The app’s name itself is a combination of two words: Cash and Kasalo, which means "to have a partner for your finances".

Fast Approval & Disbursement

Get your loans and credit approved in as fast as 8 minutes and funding to your bank account in as fast as 30 minutes!


Low Interest Rates

Easy Application Process

Cash Loan – Get cash of up to Php 7,000!

Buy Now Pay Later – Shop for gadgets, appliances, vouchers, and more; pay later in installments! *

Lazada Loan - Top up your Lazada Wallet and shop safely from home

For example:

Privacy and Permissions

Your privacy is very important to us. We will scan your government-issued ID and other information to verify your identity and Credit Profile. Your personal information will never be shared without your direct and explicit permission.


To verify your identity, we require the following app permissions:

Storage (File Access) - We use your device storage as temporary data storage during entry of your user profile and to allow you to upload images in the app

Location - We use your location and device information to help us prevent fraud, validate information you provided, and calculate/assess your credit

External Data about Other Apps - We use information about installed applications to help us prevent fraud and calculate/assess your credit
