Description

Coins.ph is Philippines No. 1 crypto wallet that allows users to buy Bitcoin (BTC), Etherum (ETH), Axie Infinity (AXS) and more. Trusted by millions of Filipinos. You can enjoy easy access to crypto spot trading pairs — from fiat-crypto or crypto-to-crypto. Sign up now, get verified and start your crypto journey with Coins!


Here are some benefits and reasons to download Coins.ph App:


COMPLIANT AND REGULATED BY BANGKO SENTRAL NG PILIPINAS (BSP)

Coins.ph is regulated by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP). We are the first ever cryptocurrency company in Asia to hold both Virtual Currency and Electronic Money Issue licenses from a central bank.


BUY AND SELL CRYPTO

Coins.ph offers a secure multi-cryptocurrency mobile wallet. You can instantly buy and sell Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Ripple (XRP), US Dollar Coin (USDC), Axie Infinity Shards (AXS), SLP (Smooth Love Potion), Tether (USDT) and more through Convert.


BE ALERTED WHEN PRICE MOVES ON CERTAIN COINS


You can set price alerts with a single tap to stay up to date on the latest prices and trends.


CASH IN AND CASH OUT FIAT TO BUY CRYPTO EASILY

You can easily add funds to your Coins Wallet via a variety of international and local payment methods. Top up fiat to buy crypto via PESONet network-approved bank transfer, Western Union international transfer, local e-wallets such as GCash, Paymaya, TouchPay and more.


User can also fund transfer to 70+ major banks (BPI, Landbank, BDO, etc.), select 5,000+ cash pickup locations (Cebuana Lhuillier, GCash, Palawan Express) and select door-to-door delivery for cash out options (via LBC Pesopak cash out).


PAY BILLS, BUY LOAD, RECEIVE REMITTANCES AND MORE

Buy prepaid loads, send money, pay bills, and receive remittances from 200+ countries - even without a bank account. Pay for your electricity, government, water, telcom and more. We support 95+ more billers in the Philippines and you can add your favorite bills and set bill reminders.


Load your BEEP card and check your card balance. Alternatively, users can also load EasyTrip NLEX and Autosweep SLEX Toll accounts. Verified users can also choose from 70+ mobile promotions from all major Philippine providers and buy international loads for prepaid phones in more than 130 countries.


Send and receive remittances through Western Union from 500,000+ locations in 200+ countries or send money to GCash, Paymaya and any major bank or remittance center in the Philippines.


BUY GAME CREDITS WITH FIAT, PLAY CRYPTO GAMES AND CASH OUT CRYPTO EARNINGS

Gaming enthusiast can buy console, mobile & PC games instantly, with or without a debit/credit card. Coins.ph Wallet supports Steam Wallet, Razer Pin, Garena Shells, Level Up! and more.


Are you a GameFi? Play-to-Earn player? You can easily cash out your SLP & AXS earnings with multiple wallet options. Transfer SLP & AXS from a Ronin Wallet Address or from an Ethereum network (ERC-20) wallet address directly to Coins.ph. Download the app to keep a lookout for more GameFi, crypto and non-crypto gaming partnerships.


SEND FIAT MONEY AND CRYPTO

Sending money via peer to peer (P2P) option from Coins.ph wallet is FREE via mobile, email or Facebook. You can also send money to any major bank or remittance center in the Philippines. Surprise your friends and family by sending a fun holiday ang pao (red envelope) via Coins app.


RESPONSIVE CUSTOMER SUPPORT

We’re always here to help, whether you’re a longtime user or just getting started. Speak to our customer support in English or Tagalog and we will make sure your queries are dealt professionally and efficiently. Email help@coins.ph.

Beep card loading on iPhone

I am just a new user of this app but I can already see how sturdy and actually appealing this app is judging by its interface. The services offered I would say is ‘just enough’ given that you have the minimum essentials but not everything is available compared to its other competitors. But what brings out the best of this app amongst others is it’s capability to virtually load your beep card through NFC which eases the hassle of bringing your card to the counter and fall into the agony of long lines just to get it reloaded. Sadly for iPhone users, this is not possible because the old iOS versions would only allow the NFC chip in the iphone to read tags. With the new iOS 13, it is already possible to read and write which means....I’m waiting for the team to have an update that would allow us to load our beep cards. By then, I’m giving this app a 5 out of 5 rating. Thanks!
