Description

**Easy sign-up**

Register without going to the branch. You can use any eligible PNB Savings or Checking account number, PNB Debit Card or Credit Card number to enroll.


**Enjoy safe and secure banking**

Login easily using Fingerprint/Touch ID or Face ID. Activate your PNB Digital Key and get added protection from the time you login and do your transactions.


**Send money anytime, anywhere**

Support your family or pay for your purchases online -. Send money between PNB accounts ,to other local banks and eWallets with InstaPay and PESONet.


**Settle your monthly bills in just one app**

Skip the lines and beat the due dates with PNB Digital’s Pay Bills feature. Pay using your debit or credit card.


**eWallet Top-Up**

Transfer funds directly to an eWallet in real-time with InstaPay.


**Speed up your transactions**

Transfer funds instantly to your family and friends by scanning their account QR Codes.


**Share your account details safely**

Create your PNB account QR Code and share it securely to your payors. No need to enter your account number anymore.


**Pay via QR code**

Scan to pay your online and in-store shops via QR Ph codes


**Manage and monitor your PNB accounts or credit cards**

View your account balances and transaction history with 24/7 real-time access to your PNB Savings or Checking accounts, or PNB Credit Cards.


**Monitor your PNB time deposits

Add and view your active time deposits easily.


**Personalize your PNB Digital App Dashboard**

Access your favorite transactions instantly by customizing your dashboard to include service features frequently used.



Download the app now and enjoy an enhanced digital banking experience!


Stay tuned as more features will be available soon!

Can’t Login on New Phone - Fixed

I’m writing this review to help others who can’t login to the PNB app when they changed phones. I used to have this problem and in fact rated this app 1 star because of this.


Apparently, you can only register your account on one phone. So on your old phone, you need to go to More > Registered Device and click the X button to remove your registered device. On your new phone, you can then sign up and follow the process to register that device for your account. Use your old login details to login at the end of the process.


If you don’t have access to your old device, visit a PNB branch and they can help you remove your old device. Hope this helps!
