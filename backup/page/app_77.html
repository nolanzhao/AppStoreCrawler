Kusog Pera is an online lending app which is dedicated to providing Filipinos with financial convenience and cash service. Its operation is easier and its loan procedure is more simplified. Kusog Pera focuses on benefiting more people who have financial needs.


How does it work?

Install the Kusog Pera app at Apple store

Register and provide your identity information

Choose loan amount and loan time

Get your loan

Increase your loan amount by repaying on time


Why choose us?

Simpler operations and processes

Faster review speed

High approval rate

Never need to worry about information security
