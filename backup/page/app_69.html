Description

Remitly is trusted by millions for sending money to family and friends overseas. Transfer money safely to 100+ countries worldwide! Remitly is secure and fast, with great rates for currency exchange. No fees for recipients, low fees for sending.


Feel safe in sending money to India, Pakistan, Philippines, Ghana, Bangladesh, Nigeria, China, Brazil, Kenya, Colombia, Nepal, Morocco, Vietnam, Poland, and more. You’ll get the exact date and time your money will arrive with your family.


Remitly helps you transfer funds worldwide using your bank account, credit card, or debit card. More money makes it home to friends and family thanks to Remitly’s great rates, special offers, and no hidden fees. Remitly provides multiple levels of security for all transfers to protect your money against fraud. Senders and recipients can talk to a real person on our Customer Care Team any hour, any day.


Get a special offer on your first transaction! Send money to 3,000+ banks and 350,000+ cash pickup locations worldwide. Home delivery options available in the Philippines, Dominican Republic and Vietnam. Send cash to 50+ mobile money providers including M-Pesa, MTN, Vodafone, eSewa, GCash, Paymaya, bKash, EasyPaisa, and GoPay.
