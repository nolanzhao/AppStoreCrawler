Description

Your new BFF, helping you one pera transaction at a time! #YouCanBayadAll


Bayad is your all-in-one pera transaction app offering accessible, easy, and reliable way of managing funds, saving, spending, and earning incentives with family and friends. Make Bayad your new BFF: Budget Friend Forever, one pera transaction at a time! Enjoy these features:


Bayad Wallet

Open a Bayad wallet, add money and you’re good to go! No need to worry, your Bayad wallet is safe and secure!


Pay Bills

Settling essential bills is just a tap away with Bayad.


Add Money

Cash-in at Bayad Centers nationwide or cash-in online through Unionbank and/or BPI.


Buy Load

Purchase or send load anytime, anywhere for any network- Smart, TNT, Globe, TM.


Send Money via QR or Mobile Number

Money transfers are now faster by just scanning a QR code or using your mobile number!


Withdraw Money

Cash-out from your Bayad wallet at select Bayad Centers!


All these and more in one app! #YouCanBayadAll
