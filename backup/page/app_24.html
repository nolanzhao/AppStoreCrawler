This new version of the Security Bank app requires an early release invite to activate. Please make sure you've received the invite from us before using this new version to avoid any issues with activation.


Our app is designed to make your life simpler. We want you to bank with confidence and save time for other important things in life.


With a few taps, you can:

- Pay Bills

- Transfer Funds

- Schedule Transactions

- Send Cash

- Reload Mobile Phones

- Locate Branches and ATMs

Glitchy, transactions doesn’t sync properly

Been a Security Bank Savings account holder for a little over two years now.


Recently got my new creditcard, but then i cant seem to log in this new app with my old credentials so i had to create a new account.


After doing so, was surprised to see my other accounts, along with my mastercard already in the new app, but not all transactions aren't showing, and there’s no way for me to monitor live what purchases are being made using my mastercard.


Went back to the old app afterwards, but then my mastercard isn't even there yet so now im using both apps.


Overall, given this lack of visibility on my account transactions especially my credit card transactions, and this buggy/glitchy app, im really considering to just use a different bank entirely for both my savings and creditcard


This app is faaaaaar from the UX ive had with other banking apps espcially UB.


Would recommend everyone to also just consider using other banks instead.
