Description

PHPocket is one of the online loan service platforms in the Philippines, which provides online loan services for Filipinos who are short of funds.

Not enough funds! No need to worry now, PHPocket solves all problems for you!

Product introduction: PHPocket is a mobile application online loan APP, focusing on providing micro-credit loan services for individuals and small business owners in the Philippines. If the money is not enough, PHPocket will help you solve and solve all your troubles caused by money.

Who can borrow?

Only need to be over 18 years old


How much can I borrow?

The higher your credit score, the more loan you can get.

Loan amount: Php2,000-Php20,000. As for the actual loan amount, you can adjust it according to your needs.

Flexible loan term options, with interest starting from 1%.

The minimum repayment period is 91 days and maximum is 120 days.

Loan interest: up to 36%/year (APR)

Transaction fees: zero


E.g:

Loan amount: Php20,000

Transaction fees: zero

Loan period: 120 days

Total payment: Php22,367 (Php20,000*36%*120/365+Php20,000=Php22,367)

The above percentages include applicable taxes.


PHPocket borrowing only requires four steps:

Download and install PHPocket, and complete the registration.

Choose the required loan amount and repayment time.

Fill in the certification information online and submit the application

Get your loan
