Description

LOG IN WITH BIOMETRICS OR 6-DIGIT PIN​

Access your app faster with these new login options. Greater convenience without compromising security.​

​

VIEW YOUR ACCOUNTS WITH EASE​

Keep tabs on all your accounts, while growing your money and making convenient everyday transactions - all from your app! It's everything you need, all in one place. ​

​

SEAMLESS SEND MONEY TO ANYONE​

Our new design and optimized screens let you Send Money with ease - whether it’s to other BDO accounts for free, or to other banks and wallets via InstaPay. ​

​

PAY BILLS WITHOUT THE FRILLS​

Beat that payment due date! Pay your bills in an instant - no need to register billers! Just enter your details and pay conveniently from wherever you are.​

​

For now, only customers with active BDO Online Banking accounts can use the new BDO Online app. Stay tuned for updates and upcoming features!
