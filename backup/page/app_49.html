Why Choose ACOM?

Legit - ACOM is one of the Leading Personal Loan Companies in Japan and is Authorized and Registered by the SEC. in the Philippines.

Lower Interest Rate - Interest rate per month is diminishing from 4.563% – 3.315% if you pay early.

Fast - Online application takes minutes. You may get the result within the day at the shortest depending on the evaluation.

Secured - We take data privacy very, very seriously and we assure you that your personal data is safe with us.

Easy Monthly Repayment – We made repaying as easy as possible requiring monthly and minimum payment just settle the amount any time prior its due date.

No Co-maker and Collateral - No need to provide any specific property or bother any person for repayment.
