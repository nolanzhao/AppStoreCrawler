Description

Bank anytime, anywhere through your phone with the Citi Mobile® App. You can download the app to your smartphone to access your account details and latest Citi offers in an instant.


With the Citi Mobile® App, you can manage your finances anytime. You can also do the following:

- View your account details.

- Pay your bills.

- Transfer funds.

- Find a Citibank ATM or Branch.


Compatible with iOS 11 or higher.


The use of this Citi PH mobile app is for the customers of Citibank, N.A., Philippine Branch and Citicorp Financial Services and Insurance Brokerage Philippines, Inc. only. Content provided via this Citi PH app is not specifically created for any territory other than where Citibank, N.A., Philippine Branch and Citicorp Financial Services and Insurance Brokerage Philippines, Inc. operate/s /is/are licensed to operate.

#1 Mobile Banking App: Financial Transactions Made Simple And Easy

Despite being a first-time user of mobile banking applications, I love how the Citi Mobile App is so easy to navigate and use. From having to visit branches all the time, I am now able to do all my financial transactions while I’m on the go or even while I’m at the comfort of my own home. I really like how I can add payees, make transfer payments, generate OTPs, while at the same time monitor my daily transactions and current balance with minimum effort required on my part. Thanks to the app, I’m also able to conveniently switch between receiving push notifications or SMS alerts to keep me updated on all my financial transactions. A big shout-out to the digital banking team of Citi PH for managing this platform which constantly provides me the convenience and peace of mind to do more each and every day. :)
