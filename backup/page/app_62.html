Description

Asia United Bank (AUB) is among the fastest-growing publicly listed universal banks in the Philippines. AUB is known for financial strength and delivery of superior innovative products and services, driven towards total customer satisfaction.


The AUB Mobile App takes off from AUB's existing online banking web application and is optimized to give AUB clients a rich, fast, and convenient mobile user experience.


Bring Banking with You with the AUB Mobile App!


Key Features:


Access your Mobile Banking Account Wherever you Are

Enroll your existing AUB Preferred Accounts to AUB’s Mobile Banking without visiting the Branch.


Transfer funds safely from home

Send funds to other AUB accounts or other Banks via InstaPay and PESONet.


Pay Bills online so you don’t need to wait in line

Experience convenient bills payment online to accredited Billers.


Your Virtual Teller On-The-Go

Queue your transactions ahead of time to use AUB’s Fast Lane when visiting any AUB branch.


PDC Management Made Easy

Manage, Re-deposit or Pull Out AUB Checks with ease without visiting the bank.


More features for the on-the-go you!

Safe and secured transactions with your Security Token, Buy and sell FX, request for Checkbook, Buy Load and more!


Manage your AUB Credit Card, any time

Access your credit card details, apply for installment programs, view promos and more!


Loan Application at your Fingertips

Experience a seamless loan application for car and big bike or apply through CASHelp for a multi-purpose salary loan.


Branch Locator

Locate the AUB Branch near you using the Branch Locator feature.


Remittance

View transaction inquiries and locate remittance centers worldwide.


Experience banking convenience with AUB!
