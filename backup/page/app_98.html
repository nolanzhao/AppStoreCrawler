Description

SALPay


This updated app is the companion to your SALPay card, a Visa debit card with an e-wallet.


You can use your SALPay card to purchase products, pay bills, manage your finances, and apply for loans at any time, any place.


With the SALPay mobile app, you can:

view your account balance and transaction history in real time;

transfer money to other SALPay accounts or to accounts with other banks;

pay your bills online;

buy prepaid credits or electronic load for cable TV, gaming, mobile phone and transportation; and

manage your account security settings.


When integrated with Salarium, the Philippines' most complete, end-to-end, cloud-based SaaS payroll software solution, SALPay helps companies disburse employee salaries more efficiently.

For more information, please visit https://www.salpay.com.
