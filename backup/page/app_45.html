Description

HelloMoney is Asia United Bank’s eWallet app. It is especially made for Filipinos to provide an easy access to digital financial services that is secure, fast and efficient.


Say Hello to a Worry-Free Life!


- Say goodbye to long lines when you go online! OPEN a prepaid bank account without the need to go to a physical branch.

- Download the HelloMoney app, REGISTER by providing the required information, take a short video and submit a valid ID. Having an ewallet is as easy as 1-2-3!

- NO initial deposit, NO maintaining balance required, NO dormancy charges.


With AUB’s HelloMoney, you can manage your funds conveniently, anytime, anywhere!


- TRANSFER FUNDS to other banks and ewallets, real time via InstaPay.

- GENERATE and SEND your own QR PH code to your friends to receive funds seamlessly without typing your account numbers.

- CASH IN or top up via 711 branches nationwide.

- Send money to your loved ones via PERA PADALA through our cash out partners (Cebuana Lhuiller, Palawan, LBC Express, M. Lhuiller and AUB branches)

- Buy PREPAID LOAD (Globe, Smart, Sun, PLDT, Meralco, DITO)

- Pay BILLS for electricity, credit cards, tuition fees, and other utilities.

- SCAN TO PAY via QR code at any QR PH P2M merchant nationwide.

- Enjoy shopping online to over a million sites using HelloMoney Virtual Mastercard

- Pair your account with an optional HelloMoney ATM card to withdraw your money from BancNet ATMs or swipe to pay via POS terminals.
