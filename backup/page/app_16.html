METROBANK MOBILE BANKING


The Metrobank Mobile Banking app gives you what you want in a banking app, plus more!


Designed around how you want to do your banking on-the-go, Metrobank gives you a mobile banking app with an easy-to-navigate and customizable interface, banking services such as balance inquiry, bills payment, and funds transfer capabilities, as well as useful services such as buying and sending load, branch locator and rate checking all on your mobile phone!


WHY USE METROBANK MOBILE BANKING


- Full online enrollment: use your Metrobank debit card or credit card to activate your account (no need to go to a branch or ATM)

- Easy payments: pay bills, buy/send load, request for payment, send out payment, and more!

- Account customization: upload profile pics for yourself and your friends, assign nicknames to your accounts

- Send or receive funds using mobile numbers: no need to disclose account numbers when transferring to anyone who is enrolled in Metrobank Mobile Banking. Request for or send out funds with personalized messages as well!

- What’s New: read about the latest in financial markets, banking news, and the latest promo deals and offers

- Forex Update: get updated foreign exchange rates

- Car and Home Loan Calculator: compute for the monthly installment required for the car or home that you want or request for assistance from a loan specialist

- Branch Locator: know where our branches are via Google Maps


Security features for your ease and peace of mind:

- Log-in with a User ID and password.*

- Receive a one-time password (OTP) and notification messages for certain transactions

- Use the Touch ID feature on enabled-devices for quick authorization of selected transactions.

- Get device authorization for every device you use


STANDARD FEATURES


Continue to enjoy basic functionalities such as:

- View account balance and details

- Pay bills/transfer funds one time or make scheduled payments/transfers

- Buy mobile/prepaid load

- Reorder checkbook


Learn more about the Metrobank Mobile Banking app at www.metrobank.com.ph/metrobankmobile
