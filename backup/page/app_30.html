Description

MANAGE AND KEEP TRACK OF YOUR ACCOUNTS

View balances and transaction history of your enrolled deposit, e-Secure Savings and credit card accounts


PAY BILLS

Pay your bills to more than 100 of your essential merchants on utilities, credit card companies, investment plans, schools and many more!

Enroll your frequent billers as Favorites so you don’t need to key in details every time.

Schedule your payments ahead of time to avoid the rush on due dates.


TRANSFER FUNDS

Transfer funds to other SBC bank accounts without the need to enroll!



Transfer funds to other InstaPay member banks or e-wallets



SEND CASH


Send cash via SMS to your loved ones wherever, whenever with eGiveCash.

Withdraw cash even without an ATM card by entering the eGiveCash number, passcode and amount.


RELOAD MOBILE PHONES

Top up prepaid load on Globe, Smart, Sun, TNT, TM.


LOCATE SBC BRANCHES AND ATMs

Find the Security Bank branch or ATM nearest you using the interactive map.


GROW SAVINGS FASTER

Earn higher interest rates with an eSecure Savings account.

Open up to 10 accounts and monitor your progress for each saving goal.
