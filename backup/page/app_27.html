Description

Taxpayers can chat with BIR personnel through the mobile application regarding concerns on TIN validation and TIN inquiry. TIN validation is for taxpayers who already have a copy of their TINs and want their respective TINs verified. On the other hand, TIN inquiry is for taxpayers who forgot their TINs.

Terrible setup but could be improved

The live service feature is a complete waste of time because no one has the time and patience to continuouly click on a button to get a hold of an agent. Since there are limited agents and plenty of clients requiring assistance, consider making the verification asynchronous where we get to submit an inquiry or validation request even if there is no agent and even outside business hours and days.
