Description

Even more features, for your joyous life!


Imagine a financial sidekick that always has your back, anytime, anywhere. We've prepared you a long list of cool and helpful features to bring your life to life!


NEED A BREATHER IN ONE STOP? CHECKED

For every bit of your lifestyle needs, we have them on the list. Train ticket, airplane ticket, digital game credits, you name it! If it's not there, then soon it will be on Livin' Sukha.


NO MORE NEEDLESS QUEUEING AT BRANCH? CHECKED

Book your schedule ahead of time then just sit back, relax, and enjoy the show till it's time to go. To the branch, obviously.


INVEST IN MUTUAL FUND SEAMLESSLY? CHECKED

Wide range of attractive mutual fund from top investment managers are now available on Livin'. Buy, sell, switch, or even compare funds' performance in just few clicks. Only on Livin'!


OPEN NEW ACCOUNT IN THE BLINK OF AN EYE? CHECKED

Literally with a blink of your eyes, your account will be ready in minutes.


QUICK PICK FOR A FLASH CLICK? CHECKED

Ever feel like a flash? Save your favorite transactions in Quick Pick.


THE HANDY INSTANT ACCESS? CHECKED

Turn on Instant Access to pay, transfer, and even check account’s balance and credit card limit on pre-login screen.


ALL WALLET, ONE LIVIN'? CHECKED

Truly beyond banking. First in banking to connect all your wallets in one app.


CARDLESS CASH WITHDRAWAL? CHECKED

Leave your wallet at home, cash out at the ATM with Livin'. It's magic.


EXPRESS FUND? CHECKED

Apply for credit card and personal loan directly from the app. Paperless. It's so digital.


Get the app now and find more features for your daily needs. All in one app!


Livin' a lively life has never been this easy.
